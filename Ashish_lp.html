Travel Checklist

Passport and Visas: Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned return date. Verify visa requirements for your destination and obtain necessary visas in advance.

Travel Insurance: Purchase comprehensive travel insurance to cover unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost luggage.

Health and Safety: Check for any required vaccinations or health advisories for your destination. Pack a first aid kit and any necessary medications.

Accommodations and Transportation: Verify your accommodation bookings and transportation arrangements. Consider pre-booking airport transfers or car rentals.
